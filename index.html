Smart City


A smart city is an urban area that leverages technology, data, and information to enhance the efficiency of city operations, improve the quality of life for its residents, and promote sustainability.


The concept of a smart city involves the integration of various technologies, such as the Internet of Things (IoT), data analytics, artificial intelligence, and connectivity, to address the challenges and complexities of urban living.
